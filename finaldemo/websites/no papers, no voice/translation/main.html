INPUT

The input of this translation machine are the sentences that are printed on Red Cards. The language chosen for the translation machine being spanish to represent the creators background.

TRANSLATION

The translation of this machine is the mold growth. The translation machine is exposed to mold spores in a moldy shed. The mold spores living in the air and walls of the shed attatch to the agar in the petri dish with no control.

OUTPUT

The output of this translation machine is the final growth of the mold on the petri dish.
